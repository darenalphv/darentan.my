About Daren

A technologist, entrepreneur, and speaker passionate about the intersection of business and innovation.

Daren Tan is a technology consultant and keynote speaker based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia — with deep expertise in digital transformation, AI strategy, and business technology.

With a rare combination of technical depth and commercial acumen, Daren has advised leading enterprises and fast-growing startups across Southeast Asia, helping them navigate the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Whether advising a Fortune 500 leadership team on their next digital move or inspiring an auditorium of entrepreneurs, Daren delivers clarity, energy, and actionable insight.
